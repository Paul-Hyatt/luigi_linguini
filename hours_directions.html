Directions

To arrive at Luigi's Linguini, you must drive to main street and take it all the way down until you reach the town's police station.

Once you reach the police station, take a right on Lincoln Avenue. Take Lincoln Avenue until you see a large sign with Luigi's Linguini and our logo. The parking lot will be to your right.

Once you are inside you will be greeted with a sign telling you to seat yourself. You may pick whatever seat looks the comfiest to you! Within five minutes of you choosing your seat, one of our waitresses or waiters will be there to help you.

Enjoy your visit to Luigi's Linguini!
